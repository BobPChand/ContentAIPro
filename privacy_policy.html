ContentAI Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

BC Canadian - USA Global Ventures LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates ContentAI Pro ("the app"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication.
  • User ID: An anonymous identifier used to manage your account and content history.
  • Product Interaction: Data about how you use the app, including content generation history and feature usage, to improve app functionality.
  • Purchase History: Subscription status and purchase records managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat to provide subscription features.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide AI content generation services through OpenAI
  • To track subscription status and enable premium features
  • To maintain your content history and brand profiles
  • To improve our app's functionality and user experience

Third-Party Services

ContentAI Pro uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Processes your content generation requests. Your prompts are sent to OpenAI to generate content. OpenAI's privacy policy is available at https://openai.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: Handles all subscription payments. We do not have access to your payment card information.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription entitlements. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data Not Collected

We do not collect:

  • Payment card numbers or financial account details (Apple handles payments)
  • Health or fitness data
  • Location data
  • Photos or videos
  • Browsing history
  • Device identifiers for advertising

Data Retention

Your content history and brand profiles are retained while your account is active. You can delete your content history within the app at any time. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account termination.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of subscription at any time through Apple's subscription management

To exercise these rights, contact us at bobchandroyalpacific@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

ContentAI Pro does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.
